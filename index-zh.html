地點

The wedding will be held at Webersburg Wine Estate in Stellenbosch, South Africa.

韋伯斯堡葡萄酒莊

莊園最多可容納 50 名客人住宿。有些房間僅供家庭使用。部分房間可能已被其他房客預訂。

莊園 (可容納 10 人)

莊園建於 1786 年，是原始的開普荷蘭建築，舖有黃色木地板。

橡樹小屋 (可容納 6 人)

橡樹小屋 (Oak Cottage) 是一間豪華的三間臥室套房小屋，設有開放式休息室、廚房和用餐區

瓊克之家 (可容納 12 人)

瓊克水宅 (Jonkershuis) 的住宿包括一套兩房公寓；兩間豪華單元配備齊全的廚房，另外兩間豪華單元配備小廚

葡萄園小屋 (可容納 26 人)

我們的葡萄園小屋坐落在葡萄園之中，可以欣賞到令人驚嘆的水壩和山脈景觀。

婚禮舉辦前約 3 個月，飯店將會向我們提供一份可用房間的清單。如果您急需入住韋伯斯堡，請直接聯絡韋伯斯堡。

斯泰倫博斯還有許多其他住宿選擇，包括其他葡萄酒農場。以及開普敦的飯店和 Airbnb。

航班和活動

航班

  • From Hong Kong: Fly direct with Cathay Pacific to OR Tambo International Airport in Johannesburg.
  • Other options from Hong Kong: Qatar Airways to Cape Town via Doha.
    Or Emirates to Cape Town via Dubai.
    Other options include Ethiopian, Qantas and Turkish Airlines, but these may have longer travel times.

  • From Johannesburg: Fly direct to Cape Town, with either FlySafair, Lift Airways, FlySAA, CemAir or Airlink.
  • From Cape Town: Use Uber or Bolt to get to Webersburg from the airport. Or rent a car from Hertz, Budget, Sani Sixt, Woodford, Avis, Tempest or Europcar.
  • Public transport is not recommended.
  • For those who may need a visa for South Africa, check the evisa website.

活動

  • There are many activities to do in Stellenbosch, including wine tasting, cycling, hiking and more.
  • Table Mountain is a must do with a beautiful view of the city and the ocean.
  • Cape Point is a wonderful national park with beautiful views that includes Simon's Town and its penguin colony. We recommend going via Chapman's Peak.
  • Robben Island was a place of imprisonment for Nelson Mandela and many other political prisoners. It is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • You can visit the South African Tourism website to find more activities.
