Location

The wedding will be held at Webersburg Wine Estate in Stellenbosch, South Africa.

Webersburg Wine Estate

The estate provides accommodation for up to 50 guests. Most rooms in the Manor House and The Jonkerhuis will be used for family.

Take a virtual tour of the estate!

Please contact Julia at Julia.Proteina@Gmail.com for room availability and bookings.

Manor house (Sleeps 10)

The manor house was built in 1786 and is an original Cape Dutch Building with yellow wood floors.

Oak Cottage (Sleeps 6)

The Oak Cottage is a luxury three bedroom en-suite cottage with an open plan lounge, kitchen and dining area.

The Jonkershuis (Sleeps 12)

The Jonkershuis accommodation consists of one two bedroom unit; two luxury units with fully equipped kitchens and additional two luxury units with small kitchenettes.

The Vineyard Cottages (Sleeps 26)

Our Vineyard Cottages are situated amongst the vineyards with breathtaking dam and mountain views.

The Webersburg website will show the venue as fully booked out for the wedding time frame, this is so we can have time to help those who want to stay there. Contact us directly so that we can book a room on your behalf and for more details.

There are many other options for accomodation in Stellenbosch, including other wine farms. As well as hotels and Airbnbs in Cape Town.

Flights & Activities

Flights

  • From Hong Kong:
    Fly direct with Cathay Pacific to OR Tambo International Airport in Johannesburg.
  • Other options from Hong Kong:
    Qatar Airways to Cape Town via Doha.
    Emirates to Cape Town via Dubai.
    Other options include Ethiopian, Qantas and Turkish Airlines, but these may have longer travel times.

  • From Johannesburg:
    Fly direct to Cape Town, with either FlySafair, Lift Airways, FlySAA, CemAir or Airlink.
  • From Cape Town:
    Use Uber or Bolt to get to Webersburg from the airport. Or rent a car from Hertz, Budget, Sani Sixt, Woodford, Avis, Tempest or Europcar.
  • Public transport is not recommended.
  • For those who may need a visa for South Africa, check the evisa website.

Activities

  • There are many activities to do in Stellenbosch, including wine tasting, cycling, hiking and more.
  • Table Mountain is one of the 7 Wonders of Nature and is a must do with a beautiful view of the city and the ocean.
  • Cape Point is a wonderful national park with beautiful views that includes Simon's Town and its penguin colony. We recommend going via Chapman's Peak.
  • Robben Island was a place of imprisonment for Nelson Mandela and many other political prisoners. It is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • You can visit the South African Tourism website to find more activities.
  • Webersburg Wine Estate offers wine tatsing, tennis and swimming facilities.
